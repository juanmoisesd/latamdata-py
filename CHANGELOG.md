## [0.1.0] - 2025-01-01

### Added
- Initial release of latamdata-py
- `load()` function for one-line dataset access
- 38 datasets from Latin America (health, neuroscience, socioeconomic)
- Support for CSV, JSON, and DataFrame output formats
- CITATION.cff for software citation
- Community health files
- .pre-commit-config.yaml with black, flake8, isort, pyupgrade
- OpenAIRE-compliant .zenodo.json metadata

### Datasets Included
- Alzheimer's disease epidemiology (Latin America)
- Mental health specialists (Ibero-America)
- Human brain connectomics
- Generative AI in academic research
- And 34 more...
