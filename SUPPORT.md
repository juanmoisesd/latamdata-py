# Support

Thank you for using **latamdata-py**! Here are your options for getting help.

## 📖 Documentation

- **README**: [github.com/juanmoisesd/latamdata-py](https://github.com/juanmoisesd/latamdata-py#readme)
- **PyPI page**: [pypi.org/project/latamdata-py](https://pypi.org/project/latamdata-py/)

## 🐛 Bug Reports

If you found a bug, please [open a bug report](https://github.com/juanmoisesd/latamdata-py/issues/new?template=bug_report.md) and include:

- A minimal reproducible example
- Your Python and latamdata-py versions (`import latamdata; print(latamdata.__version__)`)
- The full traceback

## 💡 Feature Requests

Have an idea? [Open a feature request](https://github.com/juanmoisesd/latamdata-py/issues/new?template=feature_request.md) — we welcome suggestions!

## ❓ Questions

For general usage questions:

1. Search [existing issues](https://github.com/juanmoisesd/latamdata-py/issues) first — your question may already be answered.
2. Open a [Discussion](https://github.com/juanmoisesd/latamdata-py/discussions) for open-ended questions or ideas.
