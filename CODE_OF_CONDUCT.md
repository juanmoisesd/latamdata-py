# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background.

## Our Standards

Positive: empathy, respect, constructive feedback, accountability.
Unacceptable: harassment, trolling, personal attacks, doxxing.

## Enforcement
